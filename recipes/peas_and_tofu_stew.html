Peas and Tofu Stew

One to keep the heart warm

A good mix of peas, veggies, and some smoked tofu.

Ingredients

  • Peas
  • Tofu
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Thyme
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Cut tofu into bite sized pieces.
  2. Season with some salt and pepper.
  3. Set pot on medium-high and add a spoon of oil.
  4. Make sure that the tofu bites get evenly cooked on all sides.
  5. Add diced onions.
  6. Add grated carrots.
  7. Saute the mixture.
  8. Add peas.
  9. Add water.
  10. Add tomato sauce.
  11. Add thyme.
  12. Bring to a boil and let it simmer.
  13. Allow the stew to reduce.
  14. Enjoy!

Best with

  • Pickles
