Sticky Tofu

Quick and simple

Can be served on its own, but best with rice or nooodles.

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Soy Sauce
  • Brown Sugar
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Sunflower Oil
    • can be replaced with any other frying oil

Steps

  1. Cut tofu into bite sized pieces.
  2. Season with some salt and pepper.
  3. Set pan on medium-high and add a spoon of oil.
  4. Add seasoned tofu to the pan after a minute or two.
  5. Mix 2 parts soy sauce, 1 part water, and 1 teaspoon of sugar in a small bowl.
  6. Make sure that the tofu bites get evenly cooked on all sides.
  7. Add the soy mixture to the pan.
  8. Make sure tofu bites get evenly coated.
  9. Allow the sauce to slightly thicken (caramelize).
  10. Move the sticky tofu into large bowl.
  11. Sprinkle sesame seeds on tofu and gently shake the bowl.
  12. Enjoy!

Best with

  • Basmati Rice
  • Noodles
  • Sweet Potato Bites
