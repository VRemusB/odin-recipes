Lentil Soup

Warm, spicy, flavorful

Best together with croutons and a drizzle of olive oil.

Ingredients

  • Red Lentils
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Bell Peppers
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Thyme
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Chop onions, carrots, and peppers.
  2. Add salt and pepper.
  3. Saute into a large pot.
  4. Add lentils.
  5. Add water.
  6. Add tomato sauce.
  7. Add thyme.
  8. Bring to a boil and let it simmer.
  9. Add to a blender.
  10. Enjoy!.

Best with

  • Croutons
